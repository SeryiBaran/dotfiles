## 1.23.0

Release Date: 2019-01-25

- Renamed default `babylon` parser to `babel` (requires Prettier v1.16+).
  Babel's parser, babylon, was renamed to @babel/parser in Babel 7.

  For additional info. on this change, please see:
  https://prettier.io/blog/2019/01/20/1.16.0.html#rename-babylon-parser-to-babel-5647-by-wuweiweiwu

  To read more on the Prettier v1.16 release, please see:
  https://prettier.io/blog/2019/01/20/1.16.0.html
